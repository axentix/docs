Colors

Background and text colors.

Background colors

Axentix colors are made to automatically match with the best text-color between black and white to help you getting your site the best accessibility possible. So when you choose a background color, the text will automatically be black or white, but you can change it an any time with our text-colors classes, or with your own CSS code.

Border colors

Changing the border color is really easy, you just have to specify the color with the prefix .bd-.

Dark and Light

Every color in our palette can be lighten and darken. You can light a color up to 4 times using classes .light-1 to .light-4. To make a color darker, use the .dark-1 to .dark-5 to make your color darken.
You can light and dark a text too, you just have to precise .text-dark-X or .text-light-X X as the number from 1 to 4 to light, and 1 to 5 to dark your color.
