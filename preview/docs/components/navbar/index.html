Navbar

The axentix navbar allows you to easily create a navigation menu at the top of your page.

Simple navbar

To use a simple navbar, add the .navbar class into your nav.
You can create groups of links by wrapping your links inside a .navbar-menu div.
The .navbar-brand class is useful to show your company/website name.
Use the .ml-auto class to put your menu on the right side of the navbar.
This last class is one of the margins class, which help you to move your content.

Logo navbar

To use a logo navbar, add the .navbar-logo to the <a> who contain your img. Note that the .navbar-centered class can center any element inside of your navbar.

Navbar fixed

To get a fixed navbar, simply wrap your navbar inside a .navbar-fixed container.
