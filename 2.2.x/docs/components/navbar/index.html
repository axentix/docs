Simple navbar

To use a simple navbar, add the .navbar class to your nav tag.
You can create groups of links by wrapping your links inside a .navbar-menu element.
The .navbar-brand class is useful to show your company/website name.
Use the .ml-auto class to put your menu on the right side of the navbar.
Use different margins classes to place your navbar content.

Logo navbar

To use a logo navbar, add the .navbar-logo to the <a> who contain your img. Note that the .navbar-centered class can center any element inside of your navbar.
